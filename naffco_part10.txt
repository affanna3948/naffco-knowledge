Fire Pumps & Controllers
NAFFCO offers a complete range of certified firefighting fire pump solutions worldwide including horizontal and vertical fire pumps as well as fully pre-packaged fire systems.
NAFFCO specializes in manufacturing high-performance fire pumps designed for commercial and industrial applications. Our systems deliver exceptional water flow and can be customized to meet specific building requirements with a wide range of capacities and options for electric or diesel driven units.

We provide UL, FM and LPCB approved solutions and other industrial fire pump configurations. As a trusted fire pump manufacturer in the UAE, we also offer comprehensive maintenance services to ensure long-term performance.
