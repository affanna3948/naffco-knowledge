His Highness Sheikh Maktoum bin Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum awards the “Mohammed Bin Rashid Al Maktoum Business Excellence Award” (MRM) in the field of Manufacturing across GCC to Eng. Khalid Al Khatib, NAFFCO Group CEO, along with Ahmed Khalid Al Khatib and Ali Khalid Al Khatib.
Having won the MRM Business Excellence Award 5 times in the categories of Manufacturing and Export in the free zone for more than a decade, NAFFCO’s ambition is to stay competitive and constantly on top.
Winning the MRM Business Excellence Award is an avenue that places NAFFCO on a pedestal for healthy competition for continued improvement. In 2005, NAFFCO won the MRM Business Excellence Award for the Export Category.
In 2006, NAFFCO additionally was awarded the MRM Business Excellence Award for the Manufacturing Category, advancing its way to become the best free zone manufacturing company in the UAE.
Furthermore in 2010 and 2013 NAFFCO proceeded to win the MRM Business Excellence Award for the manufacturing category in the UAE. In 2017, with another MRM victory, NAFFCO emerged a new era of success, which embraced all manufacturing companies in the GCC region.
